Email smtp:
    Create gmail account for prod and set params in .env file:
        EMAIL_HOST_USER=""
        EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD=""
        DEFAULT_FROM_EMAIL=""
    Or use other smtp and set params in .env file:
        EMAIL_HOST=""
        EMAIL_PORT=
        EMAIL_USE_TLS=""
        EMAIL_HOST_USER=""
        EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD=""
        DEFAULT_FROM_EMAIL=""
